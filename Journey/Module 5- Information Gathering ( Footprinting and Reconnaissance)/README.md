## Footprinting & Reconnaissance

Information Gathering, also known as **Footprinting and Reconnaissance**, is the **first and most critical phase** of the hacking and penetration testing lifecycle.  
This module focuses on **collecting detailed intelligence about a target organization, system, or network** before any active exploitation is attempted.

Accurate information gathering helps in understanding the **attack surface, security posture, technologies, and potential vulnerabilities** of the target.
